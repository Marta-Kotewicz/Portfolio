    • The series of photographs titled "Mergers"

    Twelve photographs depicting lake landscapes. The series focuses on the importance of the moment, the need to slow down and pause, and the importance of nature in human life. Each photograph is titled with the exact date and time it was taken, precisely down to the second, to immerse the viewer in a fleeting moment of suspension.

    The series of photographs titled "Everyday Life"

    The series explores human relationships through an everyday object. Toothbrushes are among the most intimate objects in our lives, which emphasizes the nature of the relationships depicted. The series is inspired by Gustav Klimt's painting "The Kiss."

    Hand study

    Hand study. Exploring the influence of light and shadow as well as the texture of an object.

    Shell study

    Shell study. Presenting a single object in a positive, neutral and negative light. The perception of the object changes depending on how it is presented.
