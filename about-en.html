    I completed a Master’s degree in Graphic Arts at the Faculty of Fine Arts of the Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń, as well as a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the University of Gdańsk. I have also completed two year-long, part-time specialist courses in computer graphics and interior design.

    In my artistic work, I draw inspiration from the natural world and the inner human experience. I design both vector and raster graphics. I work with typography and typesetting. I use programs such as Blender and Adobe: InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.

    I have participated in numerous group exhibitions, including international ones, as well as two solo exhibitions.
