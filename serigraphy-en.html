    • The series of serigraphs titled "Rips"

    Large-format prints exploring human behavioral patterns that often unconsciously guide our lives. These serigraphs focus on revealing the internal conflict that can arise within us as a result of these mechanisms – the contradictions between what we feel and what we think, and the often automatic emotions and reactions that stem from this tension.
